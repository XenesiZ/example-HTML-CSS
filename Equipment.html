Monster Hunter World Equipment Categories

Armor in Monster Hunter: World (MHW) is composed of Sets, Unique Pieces, Accessories and enhanced via Decorations. Monster Hunter: World is a game that focuses heavily on its Equipment, the progression will usually begin with your character dressed in puny leather armor and encourage you with each of your kills to harvest resources and craft new defensive equipment. While you may begin the game humble, by the time you reach the endgame you and your Palicoes will be donning flashy over-the-top armor and weapons and be a powerful force of nature. Most equipment typically have the look and feel of the Monsters utilized to craft each piece or set, as well as providing stat bonuses and special bonuses related to them and their elements.
