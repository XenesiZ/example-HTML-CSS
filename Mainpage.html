In Monster Hunter: World (MHW), weapons are divided into three main categories: Melee Weapons, which are close-range weapons like the Great Sword, Long Sword, and Dual Blades, focusing on direct combat and unique combos depending on playstyle; Ranged Weapons, such as the Bow, Light Bowgun (LBG), and Heavy Bowgun (HBG), which attack from a distance using arrows or specialized ammunition, often applying status effects; and finally, Special Weapons, which don’t fit neatly into either category but have unique mechanics, such as the Insect Glaive, which utilizes a Kinsect for aerial combat, and the Gunlance, which combines a lance with explosive shelling. Each type has distinct strengths and playstyles, catering to different hunter preferences.

Equipment

Armor
Talisman (Charms)

In Monster Hunter: World (MHW), Armor provides defense and unique skills that enhance a hunter’s abilities. Armor sets consist of five pieces: Helmet, Chest, Arms, Waist, and Legs, each granting different skill bonuses. Players can mix and match armor pieces to customize their builds based on playstyle and weapon choice. Additionally, Talismans (Charms) are special accessories that provide extra skill boosts, similar to armor but without affecting defense. Talismans can be upgraded to enhance their effects, allowing hunters to further optimize their skill setups. By combining armor and talismans strategically, players can maximize their efficiency in battle against powerful monsters.

Skill

Basic skills
Set Bonuses skills

In Monster Hunter: World (MHW), Basic Skills are individual abilities granted by armor pieces, charms, and decorations, providing various benefits such as increased attack power (Attack Boost), faster stamina recovery (Constitution), or elemental resistances. These skills can be stacked to higher levels for greater effects. On the other hand, Set Bonus Skills are special abilities unlocked when wearing a specific number of pieces from the same armor set. These bonuses offer powerful effects, such as Nergigante’s Hasten Recovery, which restores health with attacks, or Kushala Daora’s Kushala Blessing, which enhances wind resistance and healing. While basic skills allow for flexible customization, set bonus skills encourage players to use matching armor sets for unique advantages in battle.
