Monster Hunter World Skill Categories

Skills in Monster Hunter World (MHW) are buffs and abilities given to you by your armor, charms, and decorations. Every piece of Equipment that you have gives skill points to certain skills. There are three basic types of skills: basic skills, toggle skills, and Set Bonuses. For basic skills, the more points you have the stronger the skill becomes. Toggle skills require only one point as they are on or off. Set bonuses are special skills that you get for having a matching set of armor.
